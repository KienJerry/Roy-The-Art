My Story:


I love to imbue my drawings with elements gleaned from the fantastic tales of Greek mythology, astrology, witchcraft, and other occult lore, Gothicism medieval and Goths modern, and the unique grandeur of different cultures.

My art style focuses on female beauty in all its sinuous, alluring, unfathomable charm and strength. I explore different angles of art, attempting to harvest the pictorial riches of various periods and schools, ranging the gamut from classical to modern. I strive to fashion all these inspirations and a myriad others into something novel, something my own... and something which, I truly hope, gives as much pleasure to the viewer as it does to the creator.
