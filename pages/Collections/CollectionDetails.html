✦ GENSHIN IMPACT FANART – YAE MIKO ✦

Genshin Impact is one of the most recommended fan art collections so far and in order to depict the characters more accurately, I decided to join the journey and started exploring the game last month. It has been a great experience for me and I love immersing myself in the story and adventures of each character line in different worlds. I will try my hand at this fan art and along with the set of newly rendered posters, I challenge myself to try drawing the chibi versions and skill sets of each character.

Alternative Version

The first character of the series is Yae Miko, an electro character living in the world of Inazuma. Miko oversees the Grand Narukami Shrine and is the owner of the Yae Publishing House. Being one of high position in all of Inazuma, she is able to summon kitsune spirits or lightning strikes to destroy enemies.
